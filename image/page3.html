Pakistan's victory in the 2017 Champions Trophy final against India was historic. Key moments:

1. **Fakhar Zaman's brilliant century**: Scored 114 runs, providing Pakistan with a strong start.

2. **Mohammad Amir's lethal bowling**: Dismissed India's top order (Rohit, Kohli, Dhawan) early.

3. **Hasan Ali's outstanding bowling**: Took 3 wickets, dismantling India's batting lineup.

4. **Sarfaraz Ahmed's inspirational captaincy**: Led the team with confidence and a clear strategy, uniting the players to perform at their best.

5. **Pakistan's total**: Set a massive target of 338 runs.

6. **India all out**: Bowled out for 158 runs, Pakistan won by 180 runs.

Sarfaraz Ahmed's leadership was instrumental in this iconic win, marking one of the greatest moments in Pakistan's cricket history.
